# Art Direction

## Tone

- Dark nautical gothic
- Cursed pirate archipelago
- Gritty SNES-style action RPG readability
- Top-down pixel art

## Visual priorities

1. **Readability** — player, hazards, and interactables must read at a glance
2. **Clustered pixels** — avoid noisy single-pixel detail; form shapes with deliberate clusters
3. **Contrast** — silhouettes and palette anchors carry mood without obscuring gameplay
